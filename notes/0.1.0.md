# Frontier 0.1.0

The first stable release of Frontier — the IDE for running AI agents against your
code: many agents, across many projects, on machines you own, directed from one
place. You describe work at whatever altitude suits you (a chat message, a space
with standing instructions, a recorded meeting); Frontier gives each piece of work
an isolated place to run, streams you what's happening, and commits results as
branches you can review.

This initial stable promotes the soaked 0.1.0 nightly build, opening the stable
channel alongside nightly. Hosts on the stable channel pick it up through the
normal in-product update notice.
