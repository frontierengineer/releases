# Frontier 0.1.2

Distribution + packaging cleanup on top of 0.1.1.

- The container image is now attached to the public releases repo, alongside the
  host bundles and app installers — everything you download lives in one place.
- Internal build metadata no longer ships in the public artifacts (host bundle,
  app, and image): the closed core's package.json descriptions and .npmrc notes
  are stripped from what's distributed. Built-in extensions stay open source.

No behavioural changes to the product versus 0.1.1.
